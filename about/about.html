LET THERE BE LIVING

hello & welcome to The
Furniture Gallery

We are a team of interior designers, stylists and
professionals who share a passion for interiors and
whose mission it is to offer WA the most inspiring
furniture and decor shopping experience.

WA owned & operated, The Furniture Gallery is a family-owned company Captivating lovers of interiors since opening in 2014, we are proud to showcase a diverse collection of beautiful furniture and exquisite homewares - many of which are exclusive to us - sourced from local and international designers.

The Furniture Gallery showrooms are luxurious, state-of-the-art spaces
where clients can shop beautiful pieces in curated settings. You’ll receive
expert advice from our passionate interior stylists in store, who can help
convert your dream interior into reality.

Once you’ve found the perfect items for your home, we offer a white glove delivery service, where our experienced logistics team unveil and assemble the pieces, removing any packaging, allowing you to enjoy your new furniture without delay.

Immerse yourself in one of our galleries throughout Perth to truly appreciate The Furniture Gallery experience, or shop online with us.
